OldCraft is a weaver’s workshop from the city of Tbilisi in Georgia. Since 2011 we have been specializing in manufacturing textile for people who take interest in historical reenactment. It is this specialization that allows us to create unique fabrics and accessories perfectly matching your criteria.
The core of our work is producing wool homespun. As we sell it from stock, it means that you won’t have to wait until the order is completed.

In addition to weaving we also make clothes, cloaks and other accessories that are most suitable for festivals, hiking and other historical reenactment activities. We have chosen only the most popular and well-grounded items of clothing to match all the formal and informal rules of the historical reenactment community as much as possible.
You can check all the available items in our online store catalog
